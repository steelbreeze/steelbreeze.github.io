Landscape maps

For many years as an Enterprise Architect I have been drawing landscape maps in Visio to communicate the health of a domain's application portfolio. These diagrams are time-consuming, complex to draw and ever-changing. The landscape project provides a library that at its simplest renders a table, splitting and joining table cells, but also goes further to optimise the axes for maximum joined cells.
